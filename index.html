Welcome to Joe & Deb’s Happy Heritage Hog Farm!

Pre-order your whole happy Red Wattle hog today! Joe & Deb’s Heritage Hog Farm (J&D Heritage Hog Farm) is a family-owned and operated Red Wattle heritage hog farm located in Mountain Grove, Missouri. Nestled between beautiful mountains and shaded by oak trees, our charming farm provides spacious, well-shaded pastures where our hogs thrive. They enjoy a natural lifestyle—playing in the mud, relaxing in the grass, and munching on acorns, kitchen scraps, and feed.
